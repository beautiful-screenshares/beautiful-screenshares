How can I get better video?

Use a client with WHIP support against a service that supports WHIP.

Instead of using a Web Browser (Chrome, Safari, FireFox etc..) or App you will use dedicated broadcasting software. This software gives you greater control of what you send, and the quality of it.

Why is the quality better?

Broadcasting software gives you total control of the output video. You know exactly the video you want to send, and how you want it to look. Browsers and conferencing apps are tuned to provide the best experience for conferencing. Using WHIP from broadcasting software gives you the best experience for broadcasting/screensharing video.
